About Simple DB

Simple DB gives you access to your own database to store whatever you want via a simple web-based interface. No complicated options or hard to understand features. Just a barebones database stored on our cloud.

Databases Made Simple

Step 1: Create An Account

Simple DB is free. Create an account today and get unlimited storage space in your own custom database in the cloud!

Step 2: Create A Template

Tell us what you want to store. Receipts, invoices, timesheets, or your personal record collection. A template just needs a name and a few fields. For example, Receipt -> Date, Amount, Item

Step 3: Store Your Info

Click the Index Button to add info to your database, and click the Search button to search, view or sort! It's that simple!
